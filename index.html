About us

IEEE Computer Society Geethanjali College of Engineering and Technology Student Chapter

We have started our journey in 2018 with a motto to Grow together as Community of Technological Leaders! IEEE CS Student Chapter at Geethanjali Campus was approved on 05th November 2018 with 10 Student Members under the esteemed guidance of our Adviser Dr Somasekhar G. The Chapter was inaugrated on 24th Janauary 2019. Since inception our dynamic members have been working together to create a community of next generation leaders!

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is IEEE CS?

    The IEEE Computer Society is a growing and diverse community of computing professionals and the most trusted source for information, inspiration, and collaboration in computer science and engineering.

  • Why do students join the IEEE?

    IEEE opens the door to opportunities that will help you develop your professional identity in IEEE’s designated fields of interest: sciences, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). In addition to accessing a range of membership benefits, you’ll make global connections with people who can help you along your targeted career path through IEEE’s international community of members and volunteers.

  • How can be a committee member of IEEE CS GCET SBC?

    You need to be student of GCET and an IEEE CS member. Once you join IEEE CS, you will get involved in Chapter Activites.Based upon your volunteering spirit you can apply to be a part of various committees.

  • What am I missing by not being a member?

    ]IEEE has many, many member benefits. Members of the IEEE Computer Society receive publications of the society. Your investment in your career and your IEEE membership can be greatly increased by volunteering. See our information on volunteering to get involved.
